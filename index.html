Meet your technician

Hi, I'm Faye.

Master lash technician with over nine years behind the tweezers, and a qualified educator on top — so every set on my bed is done exactly the way I'd teach it: unrushed, precise, built to last. I run Maples Beauty & Education from my home studio on Saint Close, open five days a week with late slots on Tuesdays and Wednesdays for anyone who can't get away during the day.
